🐾 Pookie – AI Learning App for Kids

An interactive and educational mobile app that helps kids learn with fun and AI magic!

🛠 Technologies

  • Frontend: Flutter & Dart
  • Backend / AI: Firebase, Gemini API, Hugging Face
  • Features: Chatbot, voice recognition, grammar correction, kids quiz assistant

✨ Features

  • 🌟 AI chatbot using Gemini for natural kid-friendly conversations
  • 📚 Grammar correction in both English and Arabic
  • 🗣 Voice-based pronunciation assistant with feedback
  • 🎉 Fun and cute quizzes to make learning engaging
  • 🧸 AI Kids Assistant with bedtime stories, jokes, and fun facts
