A fun and interactive mobile app that helps kids learn using AI tools like chatbots, voice recognition, grammar correction, and quizzes.

Description

Pookie is designed to make learning fun and engaging for children by integrating AI-powered features:

  • Chatbot powered by Google Gemini for friendly Q&A and interactive conversations.
  • Text correction for English and Arabic with grammar and spelling help.
  • Pronunciation assistant with speech recognition and scoring for improved speaking skills.
  • Quiz mode with a cute UI to reinforce learning through play.
  • AI Kids Assistant offering bedtime stories, fun facts, and jokes to keep kids entertained.

Technologies Used

Flutter, Dart, Firebase, Google Gemini API, Hugging Face, Speech-to-text tools
