🐾 Pookie – AI Educational App for Kids

Year: 2025

Description: Pookie is a fun mobile app that helps kids learn through AI-powered features. It includes:

  • 🧠 A chatbot using Google Gemini for smart Q&A
  • 📝 Grammar and spelling correction in Arabic and English
  • 🎤 Voice pronunciation and feedback assistant
  • ❓ Quiz mode with a cute, playful UI
  • ✨ AI Kids Assistant with bedtime stories, fun facts, and jokes

Technologies: Flutter, Firebase, Google Gemini API, Hugging Face, Speech-to-Text
