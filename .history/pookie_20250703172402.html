Pookie – AI Learning App for Kids

A fun and interactive mobile app that helps kids learn using AI tools like chatbots, voice recognition, grammar correction, and cute quizzes.

← Back to Projects

Overview

Designed and developed a mobile app tailored for children to learn through playful, AI-powered features:

  • Chatbot powered by Google Gemini for friendly Q&A and engaging conversations.
  • Text correction in English and Arabic using AI grammar and spelling tools.
  • Pronunciation assistant with speech recognition and scoring.
  • Quiz mode with cute UI to make learning fun and interactive.
  • AI Kids Assistant offering bedtime stories, fun facts, and jokes.

Technologies Used

Flutter, Dart, Firebase, Google Gemini API, Hugging Face APIs, and speech-to-text tools.
