Pookie – AI Learning App

Making education fun and smart for children (2025)

About the App

Pookie is an interactive educational app designed to enhance learning experiences for kids using artificial intelligence and modern UX design.

  • AI-powered chatbot for conversations
  • Grammar correction (Arabic & English)
  • Voice recognition & pronunciation evaluation
  • Fun facts, bedtime stories, and interactive quizzes

Technologies: Flutter, Firebase, Hugging Face, Gemini API
